### About

I build practical, innovative models to understand the nutritional value and food security of global food systems – mostly working with global seafood trade data.

Lately, that’s meant spending a lot of time thinking about:
- how countries rely on seafood differently,
- where trade stabilizes food access (and where it doesn’t),
- how countries' food supplies respond to climate and economic pressure,
- and how to work with datasets that are incomplete, inconsistent, or just messy.

---

### Work

Right now I’m developing risk assessments for international seafood consumption.

The goal is to connect:
- seafood reliance  
- socio-economic structure  
- and vulnerability to climate-related exposures  

A big part of this is dealing with gaps in global trade data.  
Most of the heavy lifting is Bayesian modeling and running things on HPC systems.
